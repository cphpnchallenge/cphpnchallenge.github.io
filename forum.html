This is the place that you can post question and get answers.

  • Question 1: An object of mass 10kg undergoes uniform circular motion with a constant velocity of 10ms at a radius of 10m. How long does it take for the object to make one full revolution?
    Answer: 6.3 sec
  • Question 2: In uniform circular motion, the net force is always directed ___________.
    Answer: Toward to the center
  • Question 3: A car moves around a circular path of radius 100m at a velocity of 20ms. What is the coefficient of friction between the car and the road?
    Answer: 0.4
