Why Families Choose Bridge of Voices

Truly Accessible

Built from the ground up with accessibility in mind. High contrast modes, dyslexia-friendly fonts, screen reader support, and multiple ways to interact.

AI-Powered Patience

Our AI coach understands processing differences and waits for each child's unique pace. No rushing, no pressure, just patient support.

Family-Centered

Parents and siblings are part of the journey. Get practice suggestions, progress updates, and ways to support communication at home.

Professional Integration

Works seamlessly with speech therapists, teachers, and other professionals. Share progress and align goals across your child's support team.

Privacy & Safety First

COPPA compliant with end-to-end encryption. Your child's data and voice recordings are protected with the highest security standards.
