The Bridge of Voices club’s mission is to build “bridges” between the wide variety of voices on planet Earth. We connect people from a wide variety of cultural backgrounds, interests, and capabilities. Anyone who wishes to spread their ideas with the rest of the world may feel free to join. Whether they feel that their idea is controversial, inspirational, or simply worth sharing, anyone may share said idea to the public via this club.
